RESIDENTS

a new way to connect generations

Use the buttons below to help navigate through the pages!
Residents is a free service that helps connect users to nursing homes around the area. This service acts as the third party between potential visitors and the nursing homes, and can help to ease the process of visiting. With visiting hours, location-based searching, and information about residents, Residents hopes to bring generations together and reduce the risk of nursing home related depression.
